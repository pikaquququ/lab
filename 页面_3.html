  Boba cake (also known as postman cake) originated from Tongan County, Quanzhou, Fujian Province (today's Xiamen area, Fujian Province), and was invented by Zheng Chenggong. It is a traditional folk activity of the special extension of mooncake culture in southern Fujian. It is a game played for entertainment during the Mid-Autumn Festival. It involves tossing six dice and using the combination of results to determine prizes for participants.
